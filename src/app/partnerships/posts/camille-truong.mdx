She was also the one who gave me the push I needed to take up GitHub and commit to reproducible, version-controlled research — a shift that has genuinely changed how I work and raised the standard of everything I produce. Camille challenges me to break new ground in fungal bioinformatics, and her combination of encouragement and well-timed shoves has helped me reach levels of understanding I would not have reached alone."
