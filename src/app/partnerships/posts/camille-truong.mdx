  Camille has shaped the scientist I am today. She has a wicked knack for well‑timed criticism that has challenged me to break new ground — and I mean that as the highest compliment. Camille sparked my interest in reproducible research, encouraged me to embed myself within the global mycorrhizal research community by contributing to organisations like the International Mycorrhizal Society and SPUN, and has continued to support me to break new ground in fungal bioinformatics.
  
  Her combination of encouragement and critical feedback has helped me reach levels of understanding I would not have reached alone. With our shared passion for fungi, mycorrhizas, and conservation, I envision we have a long and fruitful research partnership ahead.
