summary: "VEAC awarded me the Bill Borthwick Student Scholarship, which supported soil physicochemical analyses for my third PhD chapter. The scholarship is named after Bill Borthwick, who in 1971 established the Land Conservation Council — VEAC's predecessor — as a body explicitly designed to insulate difficult public land management decisions from political interference. That founding principle, that good environmental outcomes require independent, evidence-based assessment, is one worth celebrating. I am proud to have received a scholarship in his name."
