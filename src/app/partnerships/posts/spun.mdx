summary: |
  SPUN exists to map and protect the mycorrhizal networks that underpin life on Earth — and they do it with a rare combination of scientific rigour, global reach, and genuine urgency. Their advocacy, media work, and commitment to supporting researchers across the global south have made them one of the most inspiring organisations in science. It is hard to overstate how much their mission has shaped and energised my own research vision.

  SPUN funded the sequencing of arbuscular mycorrhizal fungi in my PhD, for research investigating how soil nitrogen shapes mycorrhizal fungal communities across Australian temperate forests. Their support is making it possible to put Australia on the global mycorrhizal map.
